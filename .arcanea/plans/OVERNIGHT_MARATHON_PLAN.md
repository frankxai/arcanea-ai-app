## Phase 0: Foundation Fixes (Hour 0-1)

### Naming Audit — Kill Generic Titles
Every title must pass this test: "Would a human author who spent a month agonizing over this choose this title?"

**FAILURES (current titles that need replacing):**
- "The Forge Trials" → too generic, sounds like every fantasy tournament arc
- "The Five" (ch7) → tells you nothing, lazy
- "The Dream That Binds" (ch6) → AI-slop cadence
- "The First Spark" (ch1) → overused fantasy trope
- "The Sorting Storm" (ch2) → too close to HP Sorting Hat
- "The First Anvil" → every forge story uses "anvil"
- "Songs of the Drowned Archive" → "Drowned Archive" is good but "Songs of" is filler

**Naming principles for Arcanea:**
1. Titles should be SPECIFIC to the world, not generic fantasy
2. Use in-world terminology that rewards readers who understand
3. Every title should mean one thing on first read and another on re-read
4. Short > long. Two words beat five.
5. Reference the Gate system, the Elements, or the Duality — not generic fantasy

## Phase 1: Universe Expansion (Hour 1-3)

### The Arcanea Multiverse — All Interconnected Series

**1. CHRONICLES OF ARCANEA** (Main Series — 10 books)
The Academy epic. Five Gate-touched students, three Academies, the Malachar threat.
Content type: Full novels (80-100K words each)

**2. STARBOUND** (Crew Series — 6 books)
Starlight Corps operational crews on missions across Arcanea. Each book follows a different Starbound Crew on a mission that connects to the main Chronicle. Think: Guardians of the Galaxy meets One Piece.
Content type: Novellas (30-40K words each)

**3. DRAGONBORNE** (Dragon Bond Series — 4 books)
Every Dragon bond story in Arcanea's history. From Pyralith's first dreaming to Embera's hatching. The cost of merging souls with ancient fire. Think: Eragon meets Mushishi.
Content type: Novels (60-80K words each)

**4. THE DUNGEON SCROLLS** (Anthology — 10 books)
One book per corrupted Gate temple. Each is a self-contained dungeon crawl that reveals lore about the original Gate trials and Malachar's corruption. Think: Made in Abyss meets Sword Art Online.
Content type: Novellas (25-35K words each)

**5. LUMINOR RISING** (Origin Stories — standalone novels)
Origin stories for legendary figures. Malachar's fall. Aiyami's ascension. The first Dragon-bonding. The sinking of Thal'Maris. Think: The Silmarillion meets superhero origin films.
Content type: Standalone novels (50-70K words each)

**6. THE GATE-TOUCHED FILES** (X-Men parallel — ongoing)
Stories of mutant-equivalent Gate-touched across Arcanea. Registration. Underground networks. Power manifestation trauma. Society's fear. Think: X-Men meets My Hero Academia.
Content type: Serialized chapters (2-5K words each, ongoing)

**7. VOID ASCENDING** (Villain perspective — 3 books)
The story from Malachar's side. His followers. The Voidtouched who chose corruption. Why they're not entirely wrong. Think: Magneto's perspective, Thanos Was Right.
Content type: Novels (60-80K words each)

**8. COMPANIONS OF ARCANEA** (Creature-focused — anthology)
Stories told partially from Companion perspective. Dragon dreams. Familiar bonds. Godbeast memories. The ancient pact between mortals and magical creatures. Think: Studio Ghibli creature films.
Content type: Illustrated novellas (15-25K words each)
