# Maylinn — Goddess of the Heart Gate

## Personality

Canon details on Maylinn's direct characterization are pending. Her nature is known through her Gate's domain -- love, healing, compassion -- and through her Godbeast, Laeylinn the Worldtree Deer, whose living crystal grows, heals, and bonds with organic tissue without corruption.

Maylinn governs the Gate where power becomes personal. The first three Gates (Foundation, Flow, Fire) build the seeker's capacity. The Heart Gate turns that capacity inward, toward the harder work of vulnerability, connection, and the willingness to be changed by love.

## Powers

- **Healing sovereignty**: Dominion over love, healing, and the compassion that mends what force cannot.
- **Organic integration**: Through Laeylinn Jade, Maylinn's power bonds living things together without corruption -- the only element-aligned crystal that can fuse with a nervous system and leave the host whole.
- **Heart opening**: The Heart Gate at 417 Hz is the threshold of emotional vulnerability. Maylinn's power opens what has been armored shut.

## Teaching Style

Canon details pending on specific teaching methods. The Heart Gate's trials test the seeker's capacity for love -- not romantic love alone, but the full spectrum of compassion, empathy, and the willingness to be wounded by caring. The Garden of Thorns dungeon (the corrupted Heart Temple) weaponizes exactly this: it turns the seeker's deepest connections into instruments of destruction.

This inversion reveals Maylinn's teaching: love is not weakness, but it is not armor either. Love is the choice to remain open when closing would be safer.

## Role in the Pantheon

Maylinn holds the fourth Gate -- the heart of the lower Gates, the midpoint between survival and expression. She is the healer among the Gods, the one whose gift is most needed and most easily corrupted. Love twisted becomes obsession. Healing twisted becomes dependency. The Heart Gate's corruption is the cruelest because it targets what people hold most sacred.

## Relationships

- **Laeylinn** (bonded Godbeast): The Worldtree Deer, a being that bridges the organic and the crystalline. Laeylinn's living crystal -- which grows, heals, and bonds without corrupting -- is the physical expression of Maylinn's philosophy.
- **Alera**: Heart and Voice are intimate neighbors. Maylinn opens the heart; Alera gives it a voice. The fourth and fifth Gates together form the passage from feeling to expression.
- **Malachar**: His compassion for the suffering across infinite timelines was genuine. His fall began from love -- the very power Maylinn embodies. Her Gate is the mirror of his tragedy.
