# Lyria — Goddess of the Sight Gate

## Personality

Canon details on Lyria's direct characterization are pending. Her nature is known through her Gate's domain -- intuition, vision, and the ability to perceive what is hidden -- and through her Godbeast, Yumiko, whose Prism refracts perception itself to reveal things-as-they-are.

Lyria governs the sixth Gate, where the seeker learns to see beyond surfaces. After mastering survival, emotion, will, love, and truth, the Sight Gate asks: now look. Not at what is shown to you, but at what is real. The distinction between the two is the lesson.

## Powers

- **Perception sovereignty**: Dominion over intuition, vision, and the ability to see through all forms of concealment -- physical, emotional, and metaphysical.
- **Prophetic sight**: The Sight Gate is associated with prophecy and future-vision. The Athenaeum's Prophecy Spire, which breaks the ocean surface for seers to read the sky, operates within Lyria's domain.
- **Intention revelation**: Through the Yumiko Prism, Lyria's power strips away pretense and reveals hidden intentions. She sees not what you do but why you do it.

## Teaching Style

Canon details pending on specific teaching methods. The Sight Gate's trials test the seeker's willingness to see clearly -- including things they would prefer not to see. The Mirror Depths dungeon (corrupted Sight Temple) beneath Lake Clarity uses infinite reflections and future-sight overload, suggesting Lyria's teaching involves controlled exposure to overwhelming perception.

The corruption turns sight into blindness -- not the absence of vision, but the paralysis of seeing too much. This reveals Lyria's true teaching: sight without courage is its own form of blindness.

## Role in the Pantheon

Lyria holds the sixth Gate -- the first of the upper Gates, where mastery begins. The Sight Gate marks the transition from personal development (Gates 1-5) to cosmic awareness (Gates 6-10). Lyria sees what others cannot, and her gift -- or her burden -- is that this seeing cannot be turned off.

## Relationships

- **Yumiko** (bonded Godbeast): A creature whose Prism refracts perception, not light. Yumiko sees things-as-they-are, and Lyria's bond with this being reflects her own commitment to unfiltered truth.
- **Alera**: Voice and Sight are linked -- Alera names what is true, Lyria sees what is real. Together they form the perceptive capacity of the divine pantheon.
- **Elara**: Sight and Shift are the two Gates of perception -- Lyria sees from one clear perspective, Elara shifts that perspective entirely. They are complementary and sometimes contradictory.
