# Alera — Goddess of the Voice Gate

## Personality

Alera speaks with devastating clarity. Her words after Malachar's fall are among the most quoted in Arcanean canon: "We built temples to lift mortals toward the divine. Malachar made them into throats that swallow the brave and spit out bones." There is no softening in her speech, no diplomatic cushioning. She names what is true, and the naming itself carries power.

She holds the fifth Gate -- 528 Hz, the creation frequency -- the exact center of the ten Gates. This is not accidental. Truth is the fulcrum. Everything before it builds toward the courage to speak. Everything after it follows from what was spoken.

## Powers

- **Truth sovereignty**: Dominion over truth, expression, and authenticity. In Alera's presence, falsehood has no purchase.
- **Creation frequency**: The Voice Gate resonates at 528 Hz, identified across the Gate system as the creation frequency. Alera's voice does not merely describe reality -- it participates in shaping it.
- **Silence breaking**: The Voice Gate's corrupted form is silence -- stolen voices, muteness, expression suppressed. Alera's power is the antidote: the voice that speaks when every force conspires to keep it quiet.

## Teaching Style

Alera teaches through utterance. Her trials demand that the seeker speak what is true -- not what is comfortable, not what is strategic, not what is expected. The Voice Gate does not test volume or eloquence. It tests honesty. Can you say the thing that needs saying, knowing it will cost you?

Her Otome Resonite crystal shatters when lies are spoken near it. This is Alera's pedagogy made physical: the material cannot tolerate what the Gate refuses to accept.

Her corrupted temple, the Silent Spire in the Desert of Echoes, inverts truth into silence. Sound-based puzzles and stolen voices. The Gift of speech taken away.

## Role in the Pantheon

Alera holds the fifth Gate, the centerpoint of the ten. She is the Voice -- not merely one God among ten, but the one whose domain (truth, expression) is the medium through which all other Gates communicate their nature. Without voice, the other Gates are experienced but never shared.

Book 5 (The Voice Unheard) reveals that Alera's temple contains a truth the Gods tried to erase: the Gods were not always Gods. This is the most dangerous truth in Arcanea, and it lives in the Voice Gate's keeping, because Alera will not permit truth to be hidden -- even truth that threatens the divine order she belongs to.

## Relationships

- **Otome** (bonded Godbeast): A creature whose presence makes dishonesty impossible. Otome is Alera's mirror -- the living embodiment of her principle.
- **Maylinn**: Heart and Voice are paired Gates. Maylinn opens the feeling; Alera gives it form. Compassion without expression is silent suffering. Expression without compassion is cruelty.
- **Shinkami**: The truth the Voice Gate holds about the Gods' origin touches Shinkami's Source Gate directly. Alera and Shinkami hold between them the most dangerous knowledge in Arcanea.

## Key Quotes from Canon

- "We built temples to lift mortals toward the divine. Malachar made them into throats that swallow the brave and spit out bones." — Alera, addressing the Divine Council after the Fall.

## Material Correspondence
