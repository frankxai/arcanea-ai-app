# Elara — Goddess of the Shift Gate

*Note: The Shift Gate is also referred to as the Starweave Gate in some canonical sources.*

## Personality

Canon details on Elara's direct characterization are pending. Her nature is known through her Gate's domain -- perspective and transformation -- and through Vaelith Obsidian, which exists partially outside spacetime, allowing objects made from it to phase through matter. Elara occupies the liminal space between realities, the angle of vision that reveals everything previously understood to be incomplete.

The Shift Gate is where certainty goes to be tested. After seven Gates of building, opening, strengthening, loving, speaking, seeing, and understanding -- Elara says: what if you saw all of that from a different angle? What if the story you tell about your journey is not the only story?

## Powers

- **Perspective sovereignty**: Dominion over perspective, transformation, and the ability to shift between frames of reference.
- **Spacetime displacement**: Through Vaelith Obsidian, Elara's power extends partially outside normal spacetime. She can occupy positions in time and space that should not be accessible.
- **Reality phase-shifting**: The Aethervane alloy (Shift+Unity) exists in probability-state, stabilized only by perfect stillness. Elara's power is the instability -- the gift of seeing all possibilities before one is chosen.

## Teaching Style

Canon details pending on specific teaching methods. The Fractured Bridge dungeon (corrupted Shift Temple) between two mountain peaks uses reality distortion and parallel selves, suggesting Elara's teaching involves confronting the multiplicity of identity -- the recognition that the self is not one fixed thing but a pattern observed from a particular angle.

The corruption turns perspective into dissolution -- the inability to hold any single reality as true. This reveals Elara's teaching: perspective without grounding is madness. But grounding without perspective is prison.

## Role in the Pantheon

Elara holds the eighth Gate -- the perspective shift. This is the Gate where Book 8 (The Starweave) delivers its devastating revelation: Malachar speaks, and his version of history is different from the Gods'. Terrifyingly plausible. The Gods' greatest secret -- what really happened at the Source Gate -- is revealed through the Shift Gate, because only from a shifted perspective can the full truth be seen.

## Relationships

- **Vaelith** (bonded Godbeast): A being that exists partially outside spacetime. Vaelith is Elara's nature made manifest -- the creature that walks the boundary between what is and what could be.
- **Lyria**: Sight and Shift are companion Gates. Lyria sees from one clear perspective; Elara shifts that perspective entirely. Together they represent the full capacity of perception.
- **Ino**: Shift and Unity share the Aethervane alloy. Elara's instability and Ino's binding force create probability-state metal -- all possibilities held simultaneously until resolved by stillness.
- **Malachar**: The Shift Gate is where his story is retold from his own perspective. Elara's domain is the place where the enemy's version of events must be heard.
