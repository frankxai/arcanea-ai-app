# Draconia — Guardian of the Fire Gate

## What She Guards

The Fire Gate is the third threshold, where the seeker confronts power and will. Draconia guards the passage from emotion to agency -- the moment the seeker discovers that feeling deeply is not enough; one must also act, choose, and bear the consequences of choosing.

Before the corruption, her Fire Temple was "a forge of sacred flame where Draconia stood at the anvil beside each challenger, her laughter echoing off obsidian walls as she watched mortals discover strength they didn't know they had."

Now, the corrupted Fire Temple -- the Ember Labyrinth inside Mount Pyralis, beneath the Draconis Forge itself -- is a dungeon of extreme heat, flame puzzles, and rage amplification. Its Core is a black flame that burns without consuming.

## How She Tests Seekers

The Fire Gate tests whether the seeker can hold power without being consumed by it. Will is the medium. Transformation is the result.

**The trial asks**: How much can you endure before you break? And when you discover you can endure more than you believed, what will you do with that knowledge? Will power make you arrogant, or will it make you responsible?

**The corrupted inversion**: The Ember Labyrinth turns will into rage, transformation into consumption. Fire without purpose devours everything, including the one who wields it.

The original trial tested will and endurance. The corrupted trial tests whether the seeker can resist the seduction of power-without-purpose.

## What Lesson She Teaches

**Strength is not what you can destroy. It is what you choose not to.**

The Forge's motto -- "You are not made strong. You are made by the things that tried to break you" -- is Draconia's teaching. Strength is forged, not found. And the fire that forges you is the fire you choose to feed.

The Crucible -- the Forge's signature trial, where students face their worst fear made manifest -- is Draconia's pedagogy. She stands beside each challenger not to help them but to watch the moment of discovery.

## Progression Requirements

The Fire Gate is the third Gate on the path:

- **Rank threshold**: Opening Gate 3 advances the seeker to Mage rank (Gates 3-4).
- **Core lesson**: Power, will, transformation. The courage to act and the responsibility that follows.
- **Element mastery**: Fire -- energy, transformation, destruction-that-creates.
- **Dungeon**: The Ember Labyrinth inside Mount Pyralis. Extreme heat, flame puzzles, rage amplification.
- **Academy connection**: Draconia co-founded the Draconis Forge at Mount Pyralis with Draconis the Godbeast. The Forge's curriculum -- flame-forging, iron-will, dragon-bonding, shadow-walking -- is the institutional expression of the Fire Gate's teaching.
- **Critical lore**: The Forge was built on the ruins of Malachar's first fortress. His original research chambers remain sealed beneath the volcano.
