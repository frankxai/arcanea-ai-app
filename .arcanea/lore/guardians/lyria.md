# Lyria — Guardian of the Sight Gate

## What She Guards

The Sight Gate is the sixth threshold, the first of the upper Gates, where the seeker transitions from personal mastery to cosmic awareness. Lyria guards the passage from expression to perception -- the moment the seeker must learn to see beyond what is shown, to trust vision over appearance.

The corrupted Sight Temple -- the Mirror Depths beneath Lake Clarity -- is a dungeon of infinite reflections and future-sight overload. The corruption turns sight into blindness through excess rather than absence.

## How She Tests Seekers

The Sight Gate tests whether the seeker can handle seeing clearly.

**The trial asks**: Can you look at what is real -- not what you wish were real, not what you fear is real, but what actually is -- and hold steady? Can you see the truth about yourself, your relationships, your world, without flinching away? And can you see the future without being paralyzed by it?

**The corrupted inversion**: The Mirror Depths offer infinite reflections, each showing a different version of the truth. Future-sight overload -- the paralysis of seeing too many possibilities -- is the Sight Gate's corruption. Not darkness but blinding light. Not ignorance but drowning in information.

The original trial tested the courage to see. The corrupted trial tests whether the seeker can maintain clarity when perception itself becomes overwhelming.

## What Lesson She Teaches

**Sight without courage is its own form of blindness.**

The Sight Gate marks the boundary between the lower Gates (personal development) and the upper Gates (cosmic awareness). What the seeker sees from this point forward cannot be unseen. Lyria's lesson is not that seeing is good -- it is that seeing demands responsibility. To perceive clearly and do nothing is worse than not seeing at all.

The Yumiko Prism refracts perception, not light. It reveals things-as-they-are. Lyria's teaching is encoded in this property: the Sight Gate does not show you more. It shows you real.

## Progression Requirements

The Sight Gate is the sixth Gate on the path:

- **Rank threshold**: Gates 5-6 place the seeker at Master rank.
- **Core lesson**: Intuition, vision, perception. The willingness to see and the responsibility of seeing.
- **Element mastery**: Water -- the element of depth, memory, and the mirror-surface that shows what you would rather not see.
- **Dungeon**: The Mirror Depths beneath Lake Clarity. Infinite reflections, future-sight overload.
- **Alloy connection**: Veloryn (Flow+Sight) -- weapons that learn opponent patterns mid-fight; absorbs wielder's trauma. Perception made into adaptive response.
- **Book connection**: Book 6 (The Mirror Depths). A seer dreams Malachar's memories. The prophecy beneath the Athenaeum surfaces.
