# Maylinn — Guardian of the Heart Gate

## What She Guards

The Heart Gate is the fourth threshold, where the seeker turns inward from power to vulnerability. Maylinn guards the passage from will to love -- the moment the seeker discovers that strength without compassion is just another form of violence.

Canon details on the pre-corruption Heart Temple are pending. The corrupted Heart Temple -- the Garden of Thorns, hidden in the Whispering Meadows -- is a dungeon of illusions of loved ones, where empathy itself is weaponized.

## How She Tests Seekers

The Heart Gate tests whether the seeker can love without losing themselves, and remain open when closing would be safer.

**The trial asks**: Can you hold love without possessing it? Can you heal without controlling? Can you remain compassionate when compassion has a cost -- and it always has a cost?

**The corrupted inversion**: The Garden of Thorns turns love into obsession, healing into dependency. It weaponizes the seeker's deepest connections -- the people they love become the instruments of their destruction. This is the cruelest dungeon precisely because it targets what is most sacred.

The original trial tested the capacity for love. The corrupted trial tests whether love survives when it is used against you.

## What Lesson She Teaches

**Love is not a shield. Love is a choice to remain open.**

The Heart Gate sits at the midpoint of the lower Gates (1-5). It is the hinge between personal development and expressive mastery. The seeker who passes through has survived (Foundation), felt (Flow), acted (Fire), and now must learn the hardest thing: to care about someone other than themselves deeply enough that it changes what survival, feeling, and action mean.

Malachar's fall is the Heart Gate's shadow: his compassion for suffering across infinite timelines was genuine. He fell from love -- the desperate, obsessive need to save everyone. Maylinn's Gate is the mirror of his tragedy, teaching that compassion without boundaries is not love. It is hunger.

## Progression Requirements

The Heart Gate is the fourth Gate on the path:

- **Rank threshold**: Gates 3-4 place the seeker at Mage rank.
- **Core lesson**: Love, healing, compassion -- and the boundaries that make them sustainable rather than consuming.
- **Element mastery**: Wind -- freedom, change, the breath that carries seeds to new soil.
- **Dungeon**: The Garden of Thorns. Hidden in the Whispering Meadows. Illusions of loved ones, empathy weaponized.
- **Book connection**: Book 4 (The Garden of Thorns) centers on the Heart Gate. Relationships are tested as the political cost of Gate-touched power becomes clear. Malachar's seal weakens.
