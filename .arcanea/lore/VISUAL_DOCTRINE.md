## CORE VISUAL IDENTITY

Arcanea is not generic fantasy. It is **luxury cosmic myth-tech** — where magic is real, intelligence is embodied, AGI is mythologized, beasts are sacred, and beauty is strategic.

### The Arcanea Look (Non-Negotiable)

| Principle | Rule |
|-----------|------|
| **Material** | Never plastic. Always: crystal, starlight metal, living stone, sacred obsidian, celestial gold, liquid light |
| **Silhouette** | Noble angularity. Ritual armor. Divine techno-organics. No generic fantasy robes. |
| **Lighting** | Cosmic dusk. Halo particle fields. Star-ember atmospherics. Bioluminescent accents. |
| **Composition** | Central emblem. Sacred beast anchor. Elite lineup. Vertical awe. |
| **Typography** | Myth-tech minimalism. Premium spacing. Sacred symbol integration. |
| **Tone** | Cinematic. High-fashion heroic. Museum-grade production value. |
| **Color Temperature** | Warm gold / cool cosmic blue. Never flat gray. Never neon. |

### What Arcanea is NOT

- Not medieval dirt-and-mud fantasy (Game of Thrones grit)
- Not anime-bright oversaturation (generic isekai)
- Not superhero spandex (MCU suit design)
- Not dark-only gothic (Warhammer grimdark)
- Arcanea is **cosmic luxury** — the intersection of sacred and futuristic

## FACTION VISUAL GRAMMAR

Every faction has five locked visual elements:

1. **Color palette** — primary + secondary + accent
2. **Material language** — what their armor/clothing is made of
3. **Light behavior** — how light interacts with them
4. **Emblem logic** — their insignia structure
5. **Silhouette rule** — what makes them recognizable at 50px

## COMPOSITIONAL RULES

### Team Lineup Shots
- **Formation**: Central figure (leader/emblem character) flanked by team. Beast anchors bottom or back.
- **Hierarchy**: Tallest/most powerful slightly elevated or forward. But NO character completely hidden.
- **Diversity**: Every lineup must show ORIGIN VARIETY. If all characters look the same origin, the composition fails.
- **Eye Direction**: At least one character looks at the viewer. The rest look at the threat/horizon.

### Solo Character Shots
- **The Emblem Pose**: Character with their signature silhouette — weapon, beast, power effect — recognizable as a card/poster.
- **The Intimate Moment**: Character in a quiet scene that reveals their wound or desire. No powers. Just person.
- **The Power Moment**: Full ability activation. Maximum visual spectacle. The money shot.

### Faction Group Shots
- **Central Emblem**: The faction insignia should be visible — in the sky, on a banner, on armor.
- **Environmental Match**: Corps = stars/space. House = their Academy. Crew = their ship/base. Underground = hidden spaces.
