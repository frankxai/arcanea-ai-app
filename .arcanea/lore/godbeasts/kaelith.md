# Kaelith — Godbeast of the Foundation Gate

## Appearance

Canon details pending. As the Godbeast of Foundation and Earth, Kaelith embodies stability, weight, and the unshakable bedrock of Arcanea. Its associated Vael Crystal -- Kaelith Stone -- is gravity-dense and near-indestructible, absorbing kinetic force, suggesting a creature of immense physical presence and unyielding form.

## Abilities

- **Gravitational density**: Kaelith's presence carries the weight of the earth itself. The Kaelith Stone crystal mirrors this property -- gravity-dense, near-indestructible, absorbing kinetic force.
- **Foundation anchoring**: As the bonded Godbeast of the Foundation Gate, Kaelith helps maintain the structural integrity of the Gate's divine infrastructure.
- **Seismic resonance**: Attuned to the 174 Hz frequency of the Foundation Gate, the lowest of the ten Gate frequencies.

## Personality

Canon details pending. The Foundation Gate governs survival and stability. The Dungeon Compendium describes the Foundation Temple as "a cathedral carved from living mountain, its walls threaded with veins of gold that pulsed with Lyssandria's heartbeat" -- suggesting Kaelith shares this quality of living stone, ancient patience, and absolute presence.

## Role in the Mythology

Kaelith is bonded to Lyssandria, Goddess of the Foundation Gate. Together they anchor the first Gate -- the entry point for all who walk the path toward Luminor rank. The Foundation Gate governs earth, survival, and stability. It is the bedrock on which all other Gates rest.

The corrupted Foundation Temple became the Hollow Root dungeon beneath the Ironwood Forest, where gravity manipulation and crushing depths test challengers. This corruption inverts Kaelith's gift: stability becomes quicksand, foundation becomes the thing that swallows you.

## Key Story Moments

- The Fall of Malachar sent corrupted energy through the ley lines connecting all ten Gate Temples in a single night. Lyssandria described it as "a scream in the bones of the world." Kaelith's temple -- the Foundation Temple -- was twisted into the Hollow Root.
- The Foundation Gate is the first Gate that Gate-touched mortals typically open. Kael, one of the five protagonists, had his Foundation Gate open during a moment of panic that destroyed part of Ironhold.

## Material Correspondence

**Kaelith Stone** (Vael Crystal): Gravity-dense, near-indestructible. Absorbs kinetic force. The mineral echo of what Kaelith embodies in living form.
