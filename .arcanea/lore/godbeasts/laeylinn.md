# Laeylinn — Godbeast of the Heart Gate

## Appearance

Laeylinn is a Worldtree Deer -- a creature that bridges the organic and the crystalline, the animal and the arboreal. This form reflects the Heart Gate's domain of love and healing: a living being that is simultaneously a growing thing, rooted and reaching, sheltering and nourishing.

## Abilities

- **Living crystal growth**: The Laeylinn Jade crystal grows, heals its own cracks, and bonds with organic tissue without corruption. This is Laeylinn's gift made material -- the ability to heal, to integrate, to make whole without destroying what was there before.
- **Organic bonding**: Laeylinn Jade fuses with living nervous systems in what is called a Resonance Bloom -- the rarest and most irreversible of all Luminor Fragment mutations. The mutant becomes a living instrument. This reflects Laeylinn's nature as a being that joins with other life rather than standing apart from it.
- **Heart resonance**: Attuned to 417 Hz, the frequency of the Heart Gate, governing love, healing, and compassion.

## Personality

Canon details pending. The Heart Gate's domain -- love, healing, compassion -- and the Worldtree Deer form suggest a being of profound tenderness and quiet, unshakable strength. Not passive gentleness, but the kind of love that grows through stone.

## Role in the Mythology

Laeylinn is bonded to Maylinn, Goddess of the Heart Gate. Together they guard the fourth Gate -- the threshold where power becomes personal, where the seeker's journey turns inward from will and ambition toward the harder work of opening the heart.

The Heart Gate governs love, healing, and compassion. Its corrupted temple became the Garden of Thorns, hidden in the Whispering Meadows -- a dungeon that weaponizes love, turning a seeker's deepest connections into instruments of destruction.

## Key Story Moments

- Book 4 (The Garden of Thorns) centers on the Heart Gate. The Garden of Thorns dungeon targets the bonds between the cast, testing whether love can survive being turned against itself.
- The Heart Gate's corruption -- love become obsession, healing become dependency -- is among the cruelest inversions Malachar devised, precisely because the Heart Gate's gifts are the most intimate.
- Laeylinn Jade bio-interfaces are used in the most advanced Synth constructions, allowing organic tissue compatibility. This is the Heart Gate's gift extended into the Eighth Age.

## Material Correspondence

**Laeylinn Jade** (Vael Crystal): Living crystal -- grows, heals cracks, bonds with organic tissue without corruption. The physical embodiment of Laeylinn's nature. When it fuses with a living nervous system (Resonance Bloom), the mutant becomes a living instrument -- irreversibly transformed, carrying the Heart Gate's frequency in their flesh.
