# Otome — Godbeast of the Voice Gate

## Appearance

Canon details pending on specific physical form. As the Godbeast of the Voice Gate, Otome is a creature of truth and expression. The Otome Resonite crystal vibrates at the creation frequency and shatters when lies are spoken near it -- suggesting a being whose very presence makes falsehood impossible.

## Abilities

- **Creation frequency resonance**: Otome vibrates at 528 Hz, known in the Arcanean system as the frequency of truth and expression. The Otome Resonite crystal vibrates at this creation frequency.
- **Truth enforcement**: Lies spoken near Otome Resonite cause it to shatter. Otome carries this quality in living form -- a being in whose presence dishonesty cannot survive.
- **Voice amplification**: The Voice Gate governs expression and authenticity. Otome's presence strengthens the true voice of those who stand before it.

## Personality

Canon details pending. Alera, its bonded Goddess, is quoted addressing the Divine Council after Malachar's fall: "We built temples to lift mortals toward the divine. Malachar made them into throats that swallow the brave and spit out bones." This directness, this unflinching clarity of speech, likely reflects Otome's own nature -- a being that does not soften truth.

## Role in the Mythology

Otome is bonded to Alera, Goddess of the Voice Gate. Together they guard the fifth Gate -- the midpoint of the ten, where the seeker must learn to speak what is true regardless of cost.

The Voice Gate governs truth, expression, and authenticity. Its corrupted temple became the Silent Spire in the Desert of Echoes -- a dungeon of sound-based puzzles and stolen voices. The corruption inverts voice into silence, expression into muteness.

## Key Story Moments

- Alera's direct address to the Divine Council after the Fall is one of the most quoted passages in Arcanean canon, revealing the Voice Gate Goddess's character and, by extension, the nature of her bonded Godbeast.
- Book 5 (The Voice Unheard) centers on the Voice Gate. Alera's temple reveals a truth the Gods tried to erase -- the Gods were not always Gods. The Academy councils fracture under the weight of what is spoken.
- The Voice Gate sits at 528 Hz, the creation frequency -- the exact midpoint of the ten Gates. This is not accidental. Truth is the fulcrum on which all creation balances.

## Material Correspondence

**Otome Resonite** (Vael Crystal): Vibrates at the creation frequency. Lies spoken near it cause it to shatter. The material cannot tolerate falsehood -- a crystal that embodies Otome's nature so completely that its physical structure depends on the honesty of those around it.
