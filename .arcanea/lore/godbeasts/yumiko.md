# Yumiko — Godbeast of the Sight Gate

## Appearance

Canon details pending on specific physical form. As the Godbeast of the Sight Gate, Yumiko is a creature of perception and vision. The Yumiko Prism crystal refracts perception rather than light, revealing hidden intentions and things-as-they-are -- suggesting a being that sees through all surfaces to the truth beneath.

## Abilities

- **Perception refraction**: The Yumiko Prism does not bend light. It bends perception itself, showing things as they truly are. Yumiko carries this gift in living form -- in its presence, nothing can be disguised.
- **Intention revelation**: Yumiko Prism reveals hidden intentions. Yumiko's Sight Gate power strips away pretense, showing the real motivations behind words and actions.
- **Intuitive vision**: Attuned to 639 Hz, the frequency of the Sight Gate, governing intuition, vision, and the ability to perceive what is hidden.

## Personality

Canon details pending. The Sight Gate's domain -- intuition, vision, perception -- suggests a being of extraordinary awareness, one that watches with a depth that can be uncomfortable for those who prefer their surfaces undisturbed.

## Role in the Mythology

Yumiko is bonded to Lyria, Goddess of the Sight Gate. Together they guard the sixth Gate -- where the seeker learns to see beyond the visible, to trust perception over appearance, to face what their eyes have been avoiding.

The Sight Gate governs intuition, vision, and perception. Its corrupted temple became the Mirror Depths beneath Lake Clarity -- a dungeon of infinite reflections and future-sight overload. The corruption turns sight into blindness -- not the absence of vision, but the paralysis of seeing too much.

## Key Story Moments

- Book 6 (The Mirror Depths) centers on the Sight Gate. A seer among the Gate-touched begins dreaming Malachar's memories. The prophecy beneath the Athenaeum begins to surface. The cast must choose: know the future, or shape it blind.
- The Mirror Depths dungeon -- infinite reflections, each showing a different version of the truth -- is the Sight Gate's gift turned hostile. Yumiko's clarity becomes a weapon when there is too much to see.
