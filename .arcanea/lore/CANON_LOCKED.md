## TIER 7: ARCANEAN MATERIALS — THE RESONANCE SUBSTRATE (STAGING ⏳)

> *"When the Eldrians scattered, their harmony didn't dissipate — it crystallized. Every crystal, every metal, every shard is a frozen echo of what was."*

### Origin: The Luminor Fallout

When the Eldrian civilization fell, their accumulated harmonic energy condensed into physical matter — **Luminor Fragments**. Each fragment carries a residual frequency signature that determines its color, properties, and dangers.

**STAGING TRUTH:** Materials are living extensions of the cosmology — the Guardians in mineral form.

### The Three Substrate Classes

#### 1. VAEL CRYSTALS (Harmony-Locked)
Pure frequency made solid. Each resonates at a single Guardian frequency.

| Crystal | Guardian | Properties |
|---------|----------|------------|
| **Kaelith Stone** | Lyssandria | Gravity-dense, near-indestructible. Absorbs kinetic force. |
| **Veloura Glass** | Leyla | Liquid at rest, solid under pressure. Stores emotional memory. |
| **Draconis Ember** | Draconia | Perpetually warm. Amplifies willpower. Cowards cannot lift weapons forged from it. |
| **Laeylinn Jade** | Maylinn | Living crystal — grows, heals cracks, bonds with organic tissue without corruption. |
| **Otome Resonite** | Alera | Vibrates at creation frequency. Lies spoken near it cause it to shatter. |
| **Yumiko Prism** | Lyria | Refracts perception, not light. Reveals hidden intentions and things-as-they-are. |
| **Sol Quartz** | Aiyami | Stores enormous energy. Volatile. Overcharge detonates in pure white light. |
| **Vaelith Obsidian** | Elara | Exists partially outside spacetime. Objects phase through matter. |
| **Kyuro Void Crystal** | Ino | Perfectly silent. Absorbs all frequency. The material that watches without participating. |

#### 2. LUMINOR METALS (Alloyed Frequencies)
When Vael Crystals fuse under harmonic pressure, frequencies interfere — creating metals with hybrid properties.

| Metal | Alloy | Epithet | Properties |
|-------|-------|---------|------------|
| **Shael** | Foundation+Voice | "The Honest Armor" | Self-repairing, rejects deception, bonds to wearer over time |
| **Veloryn** | Flow+Sight | "Memory Silver" | Weapons learn opponent patterns mid-fight; absorbs wielder's trauma |
| **Draconite** | Fire+Crown | "Dragon's Breath" | Channels raw destruction; using it costs vitality, memory, years |
| **Aethervane** | Starweave+Unity | "Ghost Steel" | Probability-state; only perfect stillness stabilizes it |
| **Luminarch** | All Nine | "The Divine Alloy" | Theoretical. No one since the Eldrians has achieved stable nine-frequency fusion. |

#### 3. NERO SHARDS (Dissonant Fragments)
Fragments that collided, fractured, inverted — carrying Hollow Frequencies (anti-harmony).

| Shard | Properties |
|-------|------------|
| **Dissonance Ore** | Counter-frequency destabilizes Vael Crystals. Prolonged exposure causes identity dissolution. |
| **Hollow Glass** | Transparent black. Used by Silence Eaters to consume frequency from living beings. |
| **Kurusei Iron** | Alive in a wrong way — whispers, wants to be wielded. Grants power at cost of corruption. |

### Mutations: The Frequency Scars

Living beings exposed to raw Luminor Fragments undergo biological rewriting:

- **Harmonic Mutations** (Vael): Attunement to Guardian frequency, physical changes aligned to element
- **Dissonant Mutations** (Nero): Hollow Frequency syndrome, Shadow-phase, Silence Eating
- **Gray Threshold**: Both combined — most powerful, most unstable. Embody Vel'Thaan's theorem in flesh.
- **Resonance Bloom**: Rare — Laeylinn Jade fuses with nervous system. Mutant becomes a living instrument. Irreversible.

### Arcanean Meteors: The Continuing Rain

The Luminor wasn't a single event — fragments still fall. Impact types:

| Type | Nature |
|------|--------|
| **Vael Rain** | Gentle crystal dust. Flora mutates into singing forests. Civilizations grow around these sites. |
| **Ember Falls** | High-energy Draconis/Sol fragments. Explosive. War zones form around the raw power. |
| **Nero Strikes** | Dissonance-heavy. Corrupts everything in blast radius. Silence Eaters drawn like sharks to blood. |
| **Luminarch Events** | Theorized, never confirmed. All nine frequencies in unstable union. Creates a Nexus Vault or annihilates a continent. |

### Scientific Foundation

Grounded in real meteoritics: Widmanstatten crystal patterns (million-year cooling), piezoelectric resonance, schreibersite (life-seeding phosphorus), tetrataenite (rare-earth alternative alloys), tektite impact glass. The fiction layer is ONE degree of rotation: the source was intelligent civilization, not random asteroid cores.
