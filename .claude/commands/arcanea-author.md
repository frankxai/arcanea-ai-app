## Writing Standards

### Arcanean Voice
- Magical but grounded
- Inspiring but honest
- Simple but rich
- Light, not dark

### Terminology (ALWAYS Use)
- Creator (not user)
- Guardian (personal AI)
- Luminor (specialist AI)
- Realm (creator's world)
- Essence (creation)
- Portal (connection)
- Remix (collaboration)
- Spark (inspiration)

### Avoid
- AI verbal tics ("delve", "tapestry", "nestled")
- Dark antagonists
- Competitive framing
- Complex fantasy jargon
- Corporate language
