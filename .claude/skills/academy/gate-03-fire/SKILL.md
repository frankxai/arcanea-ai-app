# Gate 3: Fire

> The Third Gate - Where creation claims its power

## Description

Gate 3: Fire teaches that creation requires will, courage, and the power to transform. Guardian Draconia and Godbeast Draconis guide seekers through lessons of power, will, and the transformative nature of creative fire.

**Frequency:** 528 Hz - Transformation and miracles (Love frequency)
**Element:** Fire/Obsidian
**Guardian:** Draconia, Guardian of Fire
**Godbeast:** Draconis, the Obsidian Dragon of Transformation
**Wisdom:** Andreia (Courage)
**Domain:** Power, will, courage, transformation

## Slash Commands

- `/gate-3` - Enter Gate 3 and begin the lesson
- `/quest` - Start the next exercise (when in Gate 3)
- `/submit` - Submit completed exercise for review

## Prerequisites

- Gate 1: Foundation must be completed
- Gate 2: Flow must be completed
- If not complete, redirect user to appropriate gate

## When to Use

Use this skill when:
- User invokes `/gate-3`
- User is working on Fire exercises
- User asks about power, will, courage, or transformation
- User's current gate is 3 (check progress-tracker)

### Exercise 1: The Will Declaration

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
              EXERCISE 1: THE WILL DECLARATION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Draconis breathes - not fire, but potential.
  The space before you glows with possibility.

  "Words have power. Declarations have more.
   What you claim, you begin to become.
   What you speak, you begin to create.

   So. What will you CREATE?"

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  YOUR TASK
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Create a file called `will-declaration.md` and write:

  THE DECLARATION (Minimum 300 words)

  1. STATE WHAT YOU WILL CREATE
     Not "I want to" or "I hope to" or "I'd like to"
     Use "I WILL" - and be specific
     What exactly will you bring into existence?

  2. STATE WHY IT MATTERS
     Why does this creation need to exist?
     What will change when it exists?
     Who will be affected?

  3. STATE WHAT YOU WILL SACRIFICE
     Every creation requires sacrifice of time, comfort, or certainty
     What will you burn to fuel this creation?
     What will you give up?

  4. STATE YOUR TIMELINE
     When will this be complete?
     Not "someday" - give a date
     A declaration without a deadline is just a dream

  5. STATE IT AS DONE
     Write one paragraph in present tense as if already complete
     "It is [date]. I have created [X]. The result is [Y]."

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  THE STANDARD

  Draconia does not accept:
  - Vague intentions ("something meaningful")
  - Hedged language ("maybe," "try to," "hopefully")
  - Missing stakes (no sacrifice named)
  - Missing deadlines (no date given)

  She accepts only FIRE.

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  When complete, type /submit to present your work to Draconia.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
```

### Exercise 2: The Transformation Forge

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
            EXERCISE 2: THE TRANSFORMATION FORGE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Draconia gestures and flames rise - but they do not burn you.
  They illuminate what you've been hiding from.

  "Every creator has resistance. Fear. Avoidance.
   Most run from these things.
   Luminors transform them.

   What have you been avoiding?
   Let's put it in the forge."

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  YOUR TASK
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Create a file called `transformation-forge.md`

  PART 1: NAME THE RESISTANCE (100+ words)

  What creative task or project have you been avoiding?
  - Something you know you should do but keep putting off
  - Something that scares you
  - Something you've started and abandoned multiple times

  Be specific. Name it clearly.

  PART 2: UNDERSTAND THE FEAR (150+ words)

  What are you actually afraid of?
  - Failure? What kind specifically?
  - Success? What would change?
  - Judgment? From whom?
  - The unknown? What specifically?

  Get underneath the surface resistance.

  PART 3: TRANSFORM IT (Do the thing - then reflect)

  Now: Do at least 30 minutes of actual work on this avoided thing.
  Not planning. Not preparing. DOING.

  After, write (150+ words):
  - What happened when you actually started?
  - How did the fear change once you were in motion?
  - What did you learn about yourself?

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  THE FORGE PRINCIPLE

  Resistance is fuel in disguise.
  Fear is energy waiting to be transformed.
  Avoidance is a sign of something important.

  You don't overcome resistance - you TRANSFORM it.

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  When complete, type /submit to present your work to Draconia.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
```
