# The Creative Intelligence Workspace

I want to build a system where every creator has a persistent, intelligent workspace that grows with them.

Not a chat window that forgets. Not a note app that stores but doesn't understand. Not a gallery that displays but doesn't connect.

A system where:

- Your projects remember what you discussed last week
- Your AI assistant knows your world's lore and won't contradict it
- Your creations trace back to the conversations that inspired them
- Your style is learned, respected, and applied consistently
- Your work compounds — each session builds on every previous one

I believe AI should be the most powerful creative partner humans have ever had. Not by replacing human creativity, but by making it persistent, organized, and compounding.

The AI I work with today (Claude) can hold an 11-hour conversation and build real systems. But when the session ends, it forgets. The memory files help, but they're crude. The project graph helps, but it's new.

What I envision: a creative intelligence workspace where the AI remembers your universe, your style, your decisions, your progress — and every new session starts where the last one left off. Where your world-building is consistent across hundreds of sessions. Where your published work can be traced back through every draft, every conversation, every decision that shaped it.

That's not just a product. That's how human creativity should work in the age of AI.
