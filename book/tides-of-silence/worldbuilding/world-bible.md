# World Bible: Aethon — The World Without Shore

**Genre:** Literary Fantasy
**Tone:** Strange wonder, elegiac, sublime — beauty that contains grief

---

## The Seven Great Waters

### I. The Great Current
*"The speaking river within the silent sea."*
Warm, fast-moving water circling the world. Personality: gregarious, generative, social. Carries news, seeds, corpses, memory. Cities on the Current are cosmopolitan trading nodes. Speaks in temperature, acceleration, hull-pressure. Color: deep warm blue to viridian. Sound: cathedral organ heard from outside.

### II. The Shallows
*"Where the world is still transparent."*
Light reaches the floor. The Crowns — seamounts almost breaking surface — with hundreds of miles of reef. Intelligence is young (hundreds of thousands of years), playful, curious, eager to be heard. Children discover Tide-Speaking here. Color: every turquoise. Sound: clicks, pops, persistent murmur.

### III. The Midnight Zone
*"Past the last light, hearing begins."*
Below 1,000 feet. The most alien and ancient intelligence (~600 million years). Communicates in sensation-clusters, not messages. Practitioners who return speak slowly, find the surface noisy. Color: absolute black with hallucinatory bioluminescent flashes. Sound: sub-sonic pressure felt in chest and bones.

### IV. The Boiling Reach
*"The world making itself."*
Volcanic south. Hydrothermal vents, mineral-rich water, young basalt. Intelligence associated with creation and destruction as inseparable. Foundry cities, manufacturing nodes. Color: rust orange, mineral white, deep red algal blooms. Sound: deep irregular percussion — stone against stone, breathing vents.

### V. The Still
*"The first silence. The wrong silence."*
~400 miles across, northern hemisphere. Water from which something essential has been removed. Grey, flat, colorless. Nothing lives that isn't dying. Tide-Speakers experience it as catastrophic. The oldest records say: *"we made the Still. We do not speak of how."* Color: cold grey absence. Sound: silence as pressure.

### VI. The Glass Sea
*"Where the world became a mirror."*
Subtropical high-pressure belt. Three months of mirror-perfect surface. Intelligence is philosophical — thinks in paradoxes, shows two identical things and asks which is real. Produced the city-philosophers, the first Communion transcripts. Color: silver, sky-blue. Sound: almost inaudible hiss.

### VII. The Wandering Deep
*"The part of the ocean that has not decided where it is."*
A mile-deep zone that migrates unpredictably. The only intelligence that actively communicates curiosity about humans. Asks: *"why do you build the same shapes in new places?"* Has moods resembling enthusiasm, melancholy, concentration. Does not appear to understand anger. Sound: its own music — internal waves producing melodic resonant frequencies.

## Floating Civilization

### How Things Float
1. **Builder Polyps** — living coral grown in directed shapes over decades. Self-repairing. Last centuries.
2. **Skimmers** — domesticated gas-bag organisms, house-sized. Warm, translucent, pulsing underfoot. Last decades.
3. **Deepweed Anchors** — bioluminescent kelp thousands of feet long, tensile strength of steel cable.

### City Types
- **Drift Cities** — ride great currents, complete world circuits over years. Cosmopolitan.
- **Anchor Cities** — tethered by Deepweed. Conservative, identity tied to location.
- **Deep Cities** — sealed environments around hydrothermal vents. Bioluminescent architecture. Inhabitants physically distinct.

### Key Cities
- **Ven Thalassa** — oldest Drift City, ~40K population. The Drifting Parliament. 800 years on the Current.
- **Noor Keth** — Anchor City in the Glass Sea. 300 years. Philosophical city of deliberate speech.
- **Thuun** — Deep City in the Boiling Reach. Forge City at 700 feet. Entirely bioluminescent.

### Economy
- Calories-from-the-sea as baseline. Algae farms = grain equivalent.
- Currency: dried Vault Saury — dense, imperishable, universally valued.
- Trade flows along the Great Current.

## The Water Magic System

### Four Levels

**Listener** — Feels the ocean acutely. Knows storms before they arrive. 1 in 12 born with it. Sensation: low-frequency awareness in the chest.

**Speaker** — Interprets the ocean's signals as language. Learned skill, years of training. Cost: exhaustion of sustained attention.

**Communion** — Full perceptual immersion. Experiences through the ocean — thermal topology, creature movement miles away, geological memory. Dangerous: human perception not designed for it. Cost: loss of fine motor control, inability to focus vision, deep body cold. Cumulative changes over years.

**Convergence** — Legendary. Permanent collapse of distinction between practitioner and ocean. Two or three historical instances. Ilis Vorn, 400 years ago: the Glass Sea, when asked, says *"she is here. She is part of the speaking now. She asks me to say hello."*

### The Silence
Not absence but inaccessibility. Like a language you once knew becoming unparseable. Environmental (entering the Still) or psychological (trauma). No known cure for deep Silence. The word for this grief: *thalven*.

### Born or Learned?
Sensitivity must be present at birth. Training reveals, does not create. ~500 Communion practitioners alive at any time. Enslaved Speakers go silent. The ocean does not speak to people in chains.

## Ecology & Creatures

- **Tidecaller** — Transparent luminescent eel, 2 feet. Indicator of ocean health. Census-monitored. Disappearance is a political event.
- **Deep Sovereign** — Apex predator of the Midnight Zone. Never fully seen. Millions of years old. Humans below threshold of attention.
- **Veth (Vessel-Friend)** — Dolphin-octopus hybrid, dog-sized. Warm-blooded. Communicates in skin-bioluminescence. Chose to associate with human cities. Drawn to practicing Speakers.
- **Deepflare** — Colonial superorganism, house-sized. Renders the ocean's language in bioluminescent patterns. Flare Readings are communal practice.
- **Vault Saury** — Currency fish. 3 feet, extraordinarily dense. 40-year lifespan. "To have no Sauries" = to be truly alone.
- **Tidehound** — 8-12 foot predator. Follows ocean communication signals. Does not prey on humans. Aggressive toward those who threaten Speakers.

## Sensory Palette

**Sound:** Never quiet. Surface: constant water dialogue. Below: the hum of the Current, the sub-sonic ground tone. Cities: soft acoustic, dampened by wet wood and coral. Rain: millions of drops hiding the ocean's voice. The Still: the absence of the ground tone — you only notice it was constant when it's gone.

**Color:** Every blue. Green where biology lives. Orange/red in the Reach. Night: the world lit from below by bioluminescence. The Still: cold grey absence of color.

**Texture:** Wet wood transformed by decades — velvety grain. Living coral — hard, porous, slightly warm, feels like something paying attention. Salt on every surface. Veth skin — the smoothest surface in the known world.

**Smell:** Brine as baseline — mineral, complex, "the smell of the world." Ozone before storms. Mineral sulfur in the Reach. After rain: fresh dilution. The Still: no smell at all.

**Temperature:** Information. Warm = Current, volcanic, invitation. Cold = depth, northern, storms. Differentials are punctuation. The Still: cold from the inside, as if warmth has been replaced by a question.
