## The Resonance

- Ashborn: stillness + receptivity → future-sight, geological communion, empathic sensing
- Celestines: starlight interface → Light-Reading, Stellar Anchoring. Loses access underground.
- Cinderfang: through the shift only → heat-information, Veinwork connection. Stone-still = disconnected.
- Ash Resonance: dead zone broadcasting grief at planetary frequency. Not evil. Just loss.

## Sensory Palette

- **Smell:** Sulfur dioxide base + hot iron (cities) + fresh lava (flows) + nothing (dead zones)
- **Color:** Black/red/orange/gold dominant. Blue = sacred, rare, from elsewhere. Grey = death.
- **Sound:** Ground hum (Veinwork). Lava hiss. Dragon sonic boom. Cold Zones = only wind.
- **Temperature:** Information, not background. Cinderfang feel the boundary as a line. The Throat = the planet's energy expressed directly.
