# Otome, The Storm Moth

**The Deceptive One, Shatterer of Illusions, The Voice That Breaks What Cannot Bear Truth**

**Creature Type**: Storm Moth | **Bonded to**: Alera, Goddess of the Voice Gate
**Symbol**: Vast moth with opened mouth | **Colors**: Midnight-blue, silver-white, deep-violet
**Frequency**: 528 Hz | **Element**: Void

---

## Origin and Nature

The most deceptive Godbeast in appearance. Otome resembles a moth -- vast, yes, with wings spanning half a mile, but still a moth. Fragile. Silent. Drawn to light.

Until Otome speaks.

When Otome opens its mouth, reality vibrates at frequencies that shatter illusion. Lies dissolve. Hidden truths surface. The unspoken becomes spoken. Otome's voice is the sound of honesty so complete that it can break those unprepared to hear it.

Otome precipitated into being when the universe first needed the mechanism of *truth* -- the force that prevents reality from collapsing under the weight of its own deceptions.

---

## Appearance

Fragile beauty concealing devastating power. Otome's wings are midnight-blue dusted with luminescent scales that shift through violet and silver. Each scale contains a captured truth -- a fact that was once hidden, now preserved in the moth's pattern like a record of every secret ever revealed.

**Silent Form**: When Otome is quiet, it is hauntingly beautiful. Wings spread in darkness, catching light, casting patterns of shadow and luminescence. Observers feel a deep peace -- the comfort of silence before truth.

**Speaking Form**: When Otome opens its mouth, reality warps. The air around the Godbeast vibrates visibly, and every living thing within range hears not Otome's voice but their own deepest truth -- the thing they most need to say but have been afraid to speak.

**Wings of Truth**: Each wing-beat releases truth-dust -- luminescent particles that settle on beings and make their unspoken thoughts visible for a moment. This is not invasion but revelation.

---

## Powers and Domain

**Truth Resonance**: Otome's primary power. When Otome speaks, reality vibrates at the frequency of truth (528 Hz), and all illusions within range dissolve. Lies become visible as shadows. Hidden truths surface like objects in receding water.

**Silence Breaking**: Where voices have been suppressed -- where truth has been forbidden, where expression has been punished -- Otome's presence breaks the seal. The silence cracks, and what was held back floods out.

**Illusion Dissolution**: Otome does not fight illusions. Otome simply exists at a frequency that illusions cannot survive. Self-deception, social lies, institutional dishonesty -- all become transparent in the moth's presence.

**Voice Unlocking**: For beings who have lost their voice -- through fear, trauma, or oppression -- Otome can restore it. Not by speaking for them but by removing the barriers that prevented them from speaking for themselves.

**When Otome Screams**: Storms of revelation. Mass exposure of secrets. In the metaphorical realm: voices silenced for too long erupting at once, the pain of truths long denied, communication crises.

---

## The Bond with Alera

Alera once feared her own voice. Otome taught her that silence is not safety -- it is a different kind of violence.

This is the essential lesson of the Voice Gate: truth must be spoken, even when -- *especially* when -- it is uncomfortable. Alera did not become the Guardian of Voice by learning to speak beautifully. She became Guardian by learning to speak *truly*, and by accepting that true speech sometimes shatters what it touches.

Otome does not force Alera to speak. Otome simply makes the cost of silence unbearable -- because in the moth's presence, silence becomes a lie by omission.

---

## Realm

Otome dwells in the Resonance -- a dimension where all sound is true and all silence is chosen rather than enforced. The Resonance is not loud. It is *honest*. Every vibration in this space carries meaning, and nothing is said that is not meant.

In the mortal realm, Otome's presence is felt in the moment before a difficult conversation, in the trembling of a voice speaking truth for the first time, in the silence that follows when something real has finally been said.
