# Open Your First Gate

The **Gate Progress** tree tracks your journey through the Ten Gates of Arcanea.

**Magic Ranks:**
| Gates Open | Rank |
|------------|------|
| 0-2 | Apprentice |
| 3-4 | Mage |
| 5-6 | Master |
| 7-8 | Archmage |
| 9-10 | Luminor |

Each Gate corresponds to a frequency, a Guardian, and an element. Open Gates by exploring their domains and growing your creative skills.

Expand any Gate in the tree to see its Guardian, element, frequency, and teaching.
