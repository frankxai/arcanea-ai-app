## Where Real Revenue Lives (No Legal Issues, No Support Hell)

### Revenue Stream 1: COMPUTE / GPU ($0 overhead, pure margin)

| Product | What Happens | Revenue | Why It's Clean |
|---------|-------------|---------|----------------|
| **arcanea.ai /imagine** | User types prompt → our RunPod generates → they get art | $0.05-0.20/image or credits | They pay for GPU time, not software |
| **arcanea.ai /compose** | User describes music → Suno/AudioCraft generates | $0.10-0.50/song | They pay for compute |
| **LoRA training service** | User uploads 20 images → we train custom LoRA | $5-15/LoRA | They pay for A100 time |
| **Batch generation** | "Generate 50 Guardian variations" | $2-8/batch | Bulk compute |

**Why this works:** You're selling electricity + GPU, not software. No licenses, no EULA, no refund disputes. Usage-based = they only pay when they get value.

### Revenue Stream 2: CLOUD HOSTING (Managed Arcanea)

| Product | What | Price | Why |
|---------|------|-------|-----|
| **Arcanea Companion** | Always-on personal AI with your Guardian config | $9/mo | Railway hosting cost ~$5, margin $4 |
| **ArcaneaClaw Cloud** | 24/7 media pipeline scanning your folders | $15/mo | Railway + storage, they avoid Docker setup |
| **arcanea-orchestrator Cloud** | Managed multi-agent with dashboard | $29/mo | RunPod + Railway, saves hours of setup |

**Why this works:** The software is FREE. You charge for "we run it for you." No IP disputes. They can always self-host for free.

### Revenue Stream 3: PREMIUM CONTENT (Not Software)

| Product | What | Price | Why It's Clean |
|---------|------|-------|----------------|
| **Arcanea Academy** | Video courses: worldbuilding, agent design, prompt craft | $29-99 one-time | You're selling teaching, not code |
| **Creator Cohorts** | 4-week guided program: "Build Your Universe" | $199-499 | Live group coaching |
| **Commissioned Lore** | Custom mythology for someone's world | $500-2000 | Creative service |
| **Guardian Art Packs** | Curated, high-res, commercial-licensed art sets | $15-49/pack | They pay for curation + quality |

**Why this works:** Content and education have clear value. No recurring support obligation. One-time delivery.

### Revenue Stream 4: ECOSYSTEM COMMISSION (Marketplace)

| Product | What | Revenue | Why |
|---------|------|---------|-----|
| **Arcanea Marketplace** | Others sell skills, workflows, LoRAs, prompts | 10-15% commission | You're the platform, not the product |
| **Creator Fund** | Top creators get monthly payout from platform revenue | Retention cost | Keeps best creators on platform |

**Why this works:** Platform economics. You don't create the products, you enable the market. Low support burden.

### Revenue Stream 5: ENTERPRISE (High-Value, Low-Volume)

| Product | What | Price | Why |
|---------|------|-------|-----|
| **Consulting** | "Help us build an Arcanea-like system for our product" | $200-500/hr | Your Oracle AI Architect background |
| **Integration Support** | "Help us integrate Guardians into our AI tool" | $2K-10K project | One-time, scoped |
| **Speaking / Workshops** | AI conferences, company workshops | $2K-10K per event | Your expertise, no product support |

**Why this works:** High margin, scoped deliverables, no ongoing support obligations.

## What We DON'T Do (Avoids Pain)

| Anti-Pattern | Why We Skip It |
|-------------|----------------|
| Per-seat SaaS licenses | Support hell, churn management, refund disputes |
| Gated features behind paywall | Fragments community, breeds resentment |
| Enterprise contracts with SLAs | Legal liability, on-call obligations |
| Selling individual skills for $5 | Too cheap to matter, creates support burden per sale |
| Subscription fatigue products | Everyone hates another $19/mo |
| DRM or license keys | Technical overhead, angry customers |

**Arcanea model:** The intelligence is free. The compute, hosting, curation, and education generate revenue.
